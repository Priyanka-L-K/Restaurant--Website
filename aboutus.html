Welcome

CafeDine is a contemporary Indian bistro showcasing techniques rooted in India but influenced by our regionality, carefully sourcing only the best ingredients from both the South-Asia & Abroad.

Building off the vibrant food scene of India's Neo-bistro movement, Cafedine displays an inspiring take on vast Indian cuisine in a relaxing environment.

This enables our genuine approach to food & hospitality to shine.

Cafedine was recognized with Five star in the 2019 & 2020 Michelin guides.
